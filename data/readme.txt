Multiple perceptions:
Consists of random STFT sampling of different window length from 10 to 320 µs. The resolution is 10 pixels per µs. 

Single perceptions
No random sampling, instead the STFT responses from each sensor with window length of 410 µs are joined together to make RGB image.
